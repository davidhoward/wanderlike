A camp, a safe(ish) place to rest and craft.

Leave - back to wilderness panel. SEE: wilderness.txt
Build - build a home for yourself.
- Need to build a hearth + hut to upgrade to Home panel
- can also build a drying rack, work bench, lean-to

Rest - recover fatigue and heal. Go to rest panel. SEE: rest.txt
Craft - Do some simple crafting
